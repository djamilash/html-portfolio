Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. Starring Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hath. The film features a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of the human.

Friends with Benefits

"Friends with Benefits" is a 2011 romantic comedy film directed by Will Gluck. The movie stars Justin Timberlake and Mila Kunis as two friends who decide to add a physical aspect to their relationship without becoming a couple. As they spend more time together, they begin to develop deeper feelings for each other, complicating their initial agreement. The film explores themes of love, friendship, and the complexities of modern relationships.
It's a light-hearted and entertaining movie with great chemistry between the lead actors.
